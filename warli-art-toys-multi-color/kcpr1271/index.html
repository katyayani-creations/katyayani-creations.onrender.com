An Ancient India Tribal Art

A traditional & popular art form

Now tailor made for a child!

Warli is a traditional Indian folk art wherein white/black paint is used to make simple lines and shapes – triangles, circles and squares on a brown surface resulting in mind-blowing pictures. This valuable Warli kit introduces young children to traditional Indian Warli Art.

This kit introduces the child to this wonderful folk art. While putting together the pieces of some brown cards to form beautiful Warli art purse, wall hanging, tray as they learn the basic form. Warli art can be extended to terracotta plate & diyas resulting in some amazing creations.

  • 2 Brown colored card sheet for hand bag
  • 1 Brown colored card sheet for tray
  • 3  Brown colored card sheet for name plate
  • 4 Brown colored card sheet for coasters
  • 1 Terracotta plate
  • 2 Terracotta diya
  • Paint tub of 6 colours
  • Paint brush
  • Black sketch pen
  • Strings and easy to do instructions manual
